Books

The Hymn for the Lost Trilogy

An epic fantasy where forbidden songs hold the power to reshape reality

BOOK ONE

Song of the Forsaken

Complete

When Lirien discovers her voice can shatter more than silence, she becomes both weapon and target in a world where the Sovereignty controls every note that can be sung. In the towers of the Lyricum, she must master forbidden harmonies while navigating deadly court politics and uncovering the truth about why music was bound eight centuries ago.

BOOK TWO

Requiem for the Fallen

Complete

The songs of rebellion rise across the realm as ancient prophecies awaken. While Lirien's fate hangs in the balance, new voices join the forbidden chorus. The Deep Song stirs beneath centuries of enforced silence, and those who once served the Sovereignty must choose between the world they know and the music calling from their blood.

BOOK THREE

Hymn for the Lost

In Progress

As war threatens to tear the realm apart, Theron leads the Alliance forces while haunted by impossible memories. The Wraiths' soundless songs grow stronger, suppressors fall silent, and the prophecy's ultimate verse awaits its singers. In the battle for Vel Adryn's gates, forbidden battle hymns rise once more, and the true nature of the Deep Song will finally be revealed.

About

Lauren Fenne is a fantasy author and poet based in Melbourne, Australia. Her work explores the intersection of music and magic, weaving tales where forbidden songs hold the power to reshape reality and silence speaks louder than words.

Her debut trilogy, Hymn for the Lost, follows those who dare to sing in a world where music has been weaponized and controlled. Through her poetry collection, The Architecture of Absence, she maps the emotional geography of loss, love, and the spaces between.

When not crafting worlds where song becomes rebellion, Lauren can be found exploring Melbourne's hidden laneways, always listening for the stories that hide in the quiet moments.
